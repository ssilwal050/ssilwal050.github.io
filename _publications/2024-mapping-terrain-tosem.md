This paper provides a comprehensive survey and perspective on the use of Large Language Models (LLMs) in software engineering. We map the current landscape of research and applications, examining how LLMs are being applied across various software engineering tasks. The paper identifies key insights from existing work and provides perspectives on future directions and challenges in this rapidly evolving field.
